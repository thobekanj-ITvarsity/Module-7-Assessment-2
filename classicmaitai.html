Classic Mai Tai

Price: R20.00

Prep time: 7 mins

Yield: 3 mins

Ingredients

1 tbsp. chili powder
2 oz. dark rum
2 oz. white rum
1/4 c. freshly squeezed orange juice
Juice of 1 lime
2 oz. orange Curaçao
1 oz. orgeat syrup
Ice
2 tbsp. grenadine
Pineapple wedges, for garnish Lime slices, for garnish Maraschino cherries, for garnish

Steps

    1. Rim 2 glasses with a lime wedge. Place chili powder on a small plate and dip rim into chili powder and turn to coat.
    2. Divide rums, orange juice, lime juice, Curaçao, and orgeat syrup between glasses and stir. Fill with ice then divide grenadine between glasses.
    2. Garnish with fruit to serve.
